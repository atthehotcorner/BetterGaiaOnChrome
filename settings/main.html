    About

    BetterGaia is an add-on that changes how you experience Gaia Online. It's not a clumsy toolbar nor a useless menu. BetterGaia redesigns Gaia's layout, with your ideas in mind. We don't add bulky menus, excessive buttons, or unattractive features. BetterGaia changes how Gaia looks. A simple change like redesigning Gaia's layout does sounds small, but alltogether it makes a big impact.

    Thanks for joining users just like you that now experience Gaia in a whole new perspective. Do everyday tasks on Gaia more quickly and efficiently than before, with redesigned forums, improved site UI, and pages that just work, better.

    Think the future.
