Gaia, redesigned. Actually since 2008.

Over 4,500 people daily use BetterGaia to chat, shop, and interact better and faster than before. And with a modernized and rebuilt from the ground-up site design, navigation and more, there's no reason to miss out.

Rejoin your personality. Express yourself.

Set a header. Set a background. Style Gaia the way you like it. Choose from all the Chance Items and Event headers back since 2009, to random backgrounds, rustic logos and more. Hundreds of combinations are waiting to be made.

Talk even more. Faster and stronger.

Auto-format posts with your own post style. Don't copy and paste codes anymore. And with Instant Quoting and Editing, change and reply to posts all while staying on the same page. Type better. Redesigned formatting tools leave you frustration-free while typing.

Draw All. More collecting.

Collect Daily Chance all in one place. No more excessive page browsing or hassle, just more treasure collecting.

Notices. Even on your PC.

Stay ahead of the crowd. View your unread notifications even when you're not on Gaia, right on your desktop.

Announcement Reader. No more clutter.

No longer do you have to read announcments one by one. With one touch, view all of your announcments on the same page.
